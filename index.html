About I-5 Cleaning

I-5 Cleaning is a locally owned and operated cleaning company based right here in Longview. Founded by a Local 503 carpenter, we bring the same pride, attention to detail, and commitment to quality that comes from skilled tradeswork into every home we clean.


We’re not the biggest cleaning company in town — and that’s by design. Instead of cutting corners or rushing through jobs, we focus on doing things right the first time. We show up when we say we will, we clean thoroughly, and we treat your home with the same care and respect we’d give our own.


Clean spaces. High standards. That’s not just our tagline — it’s how we operate every single day.
